FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our logistics services.

What areas do you service in Norway?

We provide daily and weekly departures across all of Norway, with our main hub located in Stavanger. We also offer worldwide shipping services to destinations across Europe, America, and Asia.

How do I get a quote for shipping?

You can get a free quote by filling out our contact form, calling us directly, or sending an email. We'll respond within 24 hours with a detailed quote tailored to your specific needs.

Do you offer customs assistance?

Yes! We provide comprehensive customs assistance including documentation, rate negotiation, and compliance support. Our experienced team handles all the complexities of international shipping so you don't have to.

What types of goods can you transport?

We transport a wide variety of goods including commercial inventory, household items for moving, fragile items (with proper packaging), and business equipment. All shipments are fully insured for your peace of mind.

How long does delivery typically take?

Delivery times vary based on destination and service type. Within Norway, we offer same-day to 3-day delivery options. International shipments typically take 5-14 business days depending on the destination and customs processing.
